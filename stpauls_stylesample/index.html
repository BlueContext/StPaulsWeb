Supporting Colors

These colors may be used to complement the primary colors. Some of them also reflect the liturgical calendar. The logo may be used in any of the brand colors.

Neither the primary nor supporting colors should appear on top of one another. Anytime a logo or text appears in color, it should be on a light neutral background. Use black, white, or cream on a color background.

Typography

Our brand uses three primary typefaces: Caligraf, Gotham, and Book Antiqua.

Caligraf

Caligraf is a beautiful display typeface that can be used for headings and titles, but may not be available on all platforms.

Mission Statement, Headings, Sentence Case Only

Affirming that every person belongs in the beauty of holiness.

Gotham

Gotham is a clean sans-serif typeface that we use only in all-caps, tracked out to 150 / 1000. Where Gotham is not available, Montserrat may be used as a substitute.

Subheadings, Uppercase Only, 150 Tracking
